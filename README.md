# HR Analytics Dashboard — UAE Workforce Insights

A stakeholder-facing HR analytics presentation built on a dataset of **447 employees** across 3 UAE branch cities (Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah) and 7 departments, covering 2023–2026.

---

## Overview

| Total Employees | 447 |
| Average Monthly Salary | AED 23,670 |
| Average Performance Rating | 3.0 |
| Average Leave Days | 18 |
| Cities | Abu Dhabi · Dubai · Sharjah |
| Departments | IT, Finance, Admin, HR, Sales, Marketing, Operations |
| Data Period | 2023 – May 2026 |

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Headcount
- **IT** is the largest department with **103 employees**
- All 3 branch cities hold roughly equal share (~33% each)

### 2. Salaries
- **Sales** earns the highest average salary at **AED 25,025/month**
- **IT** is the lowest among top departments at **AED 22,874**, despite being the largest
- A pay equity gap of **AED 3,798** exists between Lebanese and UAE National employees

### 3. Hiring Trends
- New hires **doubled from 2023 to 2024** (75 → 148)
- Peaked in **2025 at 151 hires**
- 2026 shows 73 hires (Jan–May only)

### 4. Leave vs. Performance
- Employees with **31+ leave days** show the *highest* avg performance (3.16)
- Difference across leave bands is narrow (range: 0.21) — leave alone doesn't drive performance

### 5. Contract Workforce
- **Sharjah** has the highest contract employee concentration at **29.1%**
- Dubai is lowest at 22.5%

### 6. Top-Paying Job Titles
| Data Analyst | AED 26,353 |
| Senior Accountant | AED 25,272 |
| IT Specialist | AED 25,101 |

---

## Recommendations

1. **Salary benchmarking** — Review compensation equity between Sales and IT
2. **Pay equity audit** — Address nationality-based wage gaps
3. **Talent pipeline** — Sustain 2024–25 hiring momentum going into H2 2026
4. **Leave policy** — Data supports healthy leave; maintain current policies
5. **Sharjah compliance review** — High contract reliance warrants a benefits equity check
6. **Strategic resourcing** — Evaluate IT headcount relative to leaner departments like Operations (36 staff)
